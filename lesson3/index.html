Star Wars

Star Wars ( English : Star Wars )is a fictional universe and brandcreated by George Lucas , primarily known for its films, and has increased its reputation in comics , video games, television productions, etc. in later years. The first film in the series was released by 20th Century Fox under the name Star Wars on May 25, 1977 , and became a worldwide popular culture phenomenon. Two sequels were released three years apart. 16 years after the release of the last film of the original trilogy, the first film of the "prequel" trilogy was released, and the other two films were released three years apart. The "successor" trilogy began in 2015, and the sequels were released two years apart.According to 2019 data, the total gross of the twelve Star Wars films is approximately $10.25 billion. [ 1 ] With this revenue , the Star Wars series has become the highest-grossing film series. Star Wars has generated a massive $33 billion in revenue to date, from its films, toys, DVDs, books, computer games and merchandise.

George Lucas has been instrumental in the emergence of publications in many different areas, such as films, books, television series, video games and comics. These spin-offs, which brought many innovations to the fiction depicted in the trilogy, have enabled the formation of the fictional universe known to the followers of the series as the Expanded Universe . Thanks to these publications, the popularity and value of the Star Wars brand has been preserved in the gap between the films.
